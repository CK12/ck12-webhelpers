Notice that a rational function will either have a horizontal asymptote, an oblique asymptote or neither kind. In other words horizontal or oblique asymptotes cannot exist together for the same rational function. As gets large, values can approach a horizontal line or an oblique line but not both. On the other hand, a rational function can have any number of vertical asymptotes at the same time that it has horizontal or oblique asymptotes.
